Fast. Fresh. Flavorful. Poke Perfection.

A poke bowl is a Hawaiian dish that typically consists of rice, fresh raw fish, and a variety of delicious toppings like avocado, seaweed, and edamame. It's a healthy, customizable meal that's as visually appealing as it is tasty. Whether you're looking for a nutritious lunch, a quick dinner, or just a snack packed with fresh ingredients, PokePark has you covered. With our commitment to sustainable fishing and locally sourced produce, we ensure every bowl is both environmentally friendly and bursting with flavor.

Our Recipes

Poke bowls bring people together through their endless customizability and fresh flavors. Explore our favorite combinations below. Every recipe at PokePark is thoughtfully crafted to deliver a balance of taste, nutrition, and satisfaction. Whether you're a fan of classic flavors or adventurous enough to try something new, our menu has something for everyone.

Poke Burrito

A veggie poke bowl is packed with ingredients like fresh cucumber, avocado, seaweed, and salmon. It's a perfect option for plant-based eaters. The burrito version adds a layer of convenience, allowing you to enjoy your poke bowl on the go without sacrificing taste or texture.

Salmon Poke Bowl

Fresh salmon marinated in soy sauce and sesame oil pairs beautifully with rice, edamame, and avocado in this customer favorite. The dish combines the richness of salmon with the refreshing crunch of vegetables for a meal that's both indulgent and light for any of our customers.

Tuna Poke Bowl

Our classic tuna poke bowl features sushi-grade tuna, a savory marinade, and colorful toppings for a balanced, mouthwatering meal. It's the perfect choice for seafood lovers who appreciate the natural taste of premium fish. It is also our chef's go-to bowl, and his specialty!
